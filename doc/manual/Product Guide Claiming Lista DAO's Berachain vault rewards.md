### Product Guide: Claiming Lista DAO’s Berachain vault rewards

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*A-SMQGrE-BKBcDo9)

Lista DAO continues to bridge innovative DeFi opportunities for its community, and one exciting avenue is accessing rewards from Lista DAO’s Berachain vault. The Berachain Vault is a specialized vault for BSC users that facilitates participation in Berachain activities.

By participating in this vault through the Binance Wallet, users can earn substantial LISTA rewards. Additionally, users can also earn Boyco points through various strategies and gain access to potential airdrops from Berachain and StakeStone.

If you have participated in Lista’s Berachain Vault, this guide walks you through the simple steps to withdraw your Berachain vault rewards directly on Lista DAO’s platform, ensuring a seamless experience.

### Step-by-Step Guide to Claiming Your Rewards

Follow these steps to withdraw your Berachain vault rewards through Lista DAO:

1.  **Visit the Lista DAO Website  
    **Navigate to lista.org using a compatible browser Ensure you’re on the official site to avoid phishing scams.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*nYD7fSN5OAVnzIGm)

**2\. Access the Berachain Vault Section  
**In the dapp’s header menu, find and click on the “Vault” tab. This section displays your participation in Lista’s Berachain Vault and any associated rewards.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*W01lxDSzOhw42Vo6)

**3\. Connect Your Wallet  
**Click the “Connect Wallet” button and select your preferred EVM-compatible wallet (e.g., MetaMask). Confirm the connection by signing the transaction in your wallet.

**4\. Withdraw Your Rewards  
**In the Vault interface, click on the “Withdraw” button next to your vault position. Approve the transaction in your wallet. Once confirmed, your rewards will be transferred to your connected wallet.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*jsbbGYyGtmAx6qPW)

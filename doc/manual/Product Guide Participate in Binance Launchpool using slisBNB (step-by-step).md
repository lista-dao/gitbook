### Product Guide: How to Participate in Binance Launchpool using slisBNB

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*o0M9z7jjAa9K5CDG)

Binance Launchpool offers unique opportunities to earn rewards by staking BNB or FDUSD on the platform.

Recently, Binance announced that users holding slisBNB are now eligible to participate in upcoming and future Launchpools, allowing users to increase their earning potential while maintaining liquidity.

This product guide walks you through how to participate in Binance Launchpool using slisBNB. Whether you're an experienced DeFi enthusiast or new to the space, this guide will help you complete the process with ease and confidence.

### How to qualify for Binance Launchpool participation

The following are the terms and conditions for participating in Binance Launchpool with slisBNB:

1. Hold slisBNB.
2. The holding wallet must be a Binance Web3 Wallet.

### How to get a Binance Web3 Wallet

If you don't have a Binance Web3 Wallet yet, follow the [guide here](https://academy.binance.com/en/articles/what-is-binance-web3-wallet-and-how-does-it-work) to learn how to create one.

**There are two ways to get slisBNB in your Binance Web3 Wallet:**

1. Liquid stake BNB into slisBNB.
2. Swap directly for slisBNB.

Before we begin, you need to deposit BNB in your Binance Web3 Wallet to pay for gas fees.

Here’s what you can do:

1. Log into the Binance app and go to the "Wallet" section. Open "Web3".

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*hrnh1KE24azNUgcO)

2. To fund your wallet, tap "Receive". If you’re transferring from your Binance account, tap "Transfer".

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*qKQP7OdE47uEVywE)

3. Select the asset to transfer (BNB in this example) and choose the BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20) network for the transfer.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*pA0ZGNdGErD09YZN)

4. Enter the amount of BNB you want to send to your Binance Web3 Wallet, then tap Withdraw.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*XQ2HvZbhjdDby8Hk)

### Option 1: Liquid stake BNB into slisBNB on Lista DAO

Once your wallet is funded with BNB, you can liquid stake your BNB to receive slisBNB.

Here’s what you can do:

1. Go to the "Earn" section in the bottom right.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*GoRGFVZofK-vNuh8)

2. Under Lista DAO, tap "Stake".

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*y_FboUwDJCvWWLfI)

3. You’ll be taken to Lista DAO’s page in the app. Connect your Binance Web3 Wallet, then tap "Stake".

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*9CAJIOnDD3JdhTyD)

4. Enter the amount of BNB you want to liquid stake on Lista. Remember to leave some BNB for gas!

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*35DkqljznJYz4FWu)

5. Confirm the transaction. Once your BNB is liquid staked on Lista DAO, you’ll see a confirmation.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*vVWvPBHEZ1I6l1CS)

6. After the transaction is confirmed, you’re done.

### Option 2: Swap directly for slisBNB

Alternatively, you can get slisBNB by swapping your assets directly for it.

Here’s what you can do:

1. Log into your Binance Web3 Wallet.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*bNSIq_dyBtyUkbqL)

2. In the Binance Web3 Wallet, tap the "Swap" tab at the bottom.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*OXh-1e8UDxnaosec)

3. Select the asset you want to swap for slisBNB. In the screenshot we use BNB as an example:

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*NO9h9X0UbV7K0fz3)

4. Enter the amount of BNB you want to swap (e.g. 0.1 BNB as in the image). In the "Swap to" section, select slisBNB from the dropdown.

5. Check the details to make sure they’re correct.

6. Tap Swap to start the transaction.

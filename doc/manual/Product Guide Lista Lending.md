### Product Guide: Lista Lending

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*VRy7FOUF214Xc_q0)

Lista Lending is a decentralized, vault-based lending protocol on BNB Chain, designed to optimize capital efficiency and enable flexible peer-to-peer lending. Users can supply assets to earn yield, borrow against collateral with dynamic rates via the AdaptiveCurveIRM.

This guide walks you through using the [Lista Lending DApp](https://lista.org/lending), covering key actions for lenders, borrowers, and vault curators.

### Prerequisites

Before using Lista Lending, ensure you have:

*   **BNB Chain Wallet**: A wallet like MetaMask configured for BNB Chain with BNB for gas fees.
*   **Assets**: Tokens supported by Lista Lending (e.g., BNB, slisBNB, lisUSD, pt-clisBNB).
*   **Basic DeFi Knowledge**: Understanding of lending, borrowing, and liquidation risks.

### Step 1: Connect to the dApp

1.  **Visit the dApp**: Go to [https://lista.org/lending](https://lista.org/lending).

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*BcLlPPaZneyjRj0j)

1.  **Connect Wallet**:

*   Click “Connect Wallet” (typically in the header or homepage).
*   Select your wallet (e.g., MetaMask) and approve the connection.
*   Ensure your wallet is set to BNB Chain (Chain ID: 56).

**2\. View Dashboard**:

*   Once connected, you’ll see options like “Earn” (for lending) and “Borrow” (for borrowing).

### Step 2: Supply Assets (Lending)

Lenders deposit assets into vaults to earn a vault-weighted Supply APY.

#### How to Supply

1.  **Navigate to Earn Page**: From the homepage, click “Earn” (first screen) or via the header.
2.  **Select a Vault**:

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*Zs0xLUPNeNqD_KSR)

*   Browse the Vault List (sorted by Deposit or APY, 10 per page).
*   View details: Vault Name, Deposit Amount (e.g., xxx BNB/USD), APY, Collateral Types.
*   Choose a vault and click on it.

**3\. Deposit Assets**:

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*fheSBgBcbHi4oxUK)

*   Click the vault to enter its Vault Page.
*   In the Deposit Section, input the amount (e.g., 10 BNB) or click “Max.”
*   Check APY and estimated profits (Monthly: (Position + Input) × APY / 12; Yearly: (Position + Input) × APY).
*   Approve the token spend, then confirm the deposit transaction.

**4\. Monitor Earnings**:

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*CD27Q0BCVmW0oZAc)

*   Return to Lista Lending to view your dashboard.
*   View Total Earn Assets (excludes collateral), APY curve (7d/1m/3m/all), and Lista rewards (claim via button).

**5\. Withdraw asset**

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*ZZQt80im1GtCAy5q)

*   Click the vault to enter its Vault Page.
*   In the right section, click on Withdraw, input the amount (e.g., 10 BNB) or click “Withdraw all”
*   Confirm the deposit transaction and you will receive your BNB.

### Step 3: Borrow Assets

Borrowers collateralize assets to access loans, with rates set by the AdaptiveCurveIRM.

#### How to Borrow

**1\. Navigate to Borrow Page**:   
Click “Borrow” from the Lista Lending homepage.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*lLgnMY2yVRyLW5sK)

**2\. Choose Collateral**:

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*UAdZF8U_BOh6bovJ)

*   In the Collateral List, filter by asset (e.g., slisBNB, BNB) or view all.
*   Check details: Collateral Name, Loan Token (e.g., WBNB), LLTV, Borrowable Amount (liquidity), borrow Rate.
*   Select a market (e.g. BTCB).

**3\. Deposit Collateral and Borrow**

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*qtUk0aj4Pt6sEKJ0)

*   On the Collateral Page, input the amount of collateral to supply (e.g., BTCB) in the supply collateral section.
*   Specify the borrow amount (e.g., WBNB) within the Borrowable WBNB section.
*   Review the Rate (e.g., 4% at 90% utilization) and confirm the transaction.
*   Approve and confirm the deposit transaction.

**4\. Monitor Debt**:

*   In the borrow page, click on My position to monitor your debt.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*oYkyi33j_eMtnRwh)

**5\. Withdraw (Lenders)**:

*   Go to Earn Page → Operation Section.
*   Input amount, confirm withdrawal (subject to liquidity).

**6\. Repay (Borrowers)**:

*   Go to Borrow Page → Debt Details.
*   Repay principal + interest, reclaim collateral.

What’s New: Minting $clisBNB with BNB/slisBNB LP Tokens
=======================================================

Our latest update allows users to mint $clisBNB by staking BNB/slisBNB LP tokens. This integration combines the benefits of liquidity provision with Lista DAO’s advanced lending and staking mechanisms, offering a seamless way to amplify your yields. Here’s what this feature enables:

*   **Stake BNB/slisBNB LP Tokens**: Provide liquidity for the slisBNB/BNB pair on ThenaFi, and stake your LP tokens on Lista DAO.
*   **Mint $clisBNB**: Use your staked LP tokens to mint $clisBNB, which can be utilized in Lista’s ecosystem for further strategies.

This update reflects Lista DAO’s commitment to capital efficiency, allowing users to stack multiple yield streams through Binance Launch pool while maintaining exposure to BNB’s staking rewards through slisBNB.

**Step-by-Step Guide: How to Mint $clisBNB**

1.  **Provide Liquidity on ThenaFi**:

*   Visit the ThenaFi LP pool for slisBNB/BNB at [ThenaFi Pool](https://thena.fi/pools/0xa07bbf09b48e8d219774ac9b92622f5260a9c9f4).
*   Connect your wallet (e.g., MetaMask or WalletConnect).
*   Deposit an equal value of slisBNB and BNB to create your LP tokens.
*   Confirm the transaction in your wallet. You’ll now hold slisBNB/BNB LP tokens and start earning farming rewards.

**2\. Stake LP Tokens on Lista DAO to Mint $clisBNB**:

*   Head to Lista DAO’s platform at [lista.org](https://lista.org) and navigate to the “My slisBNB LP” section

*   Click on the “Stake” button under “slisBNB/BNB Stable LP” to stake your LP tokens in the Staked Vault.
*   Once staked, you’ll automatically mint $clisBNB, which will appear under “My $clisBNB Minted by LP.” You can choose to delegate this $clisBNB to another wallet address by clicking the “Manage” button.
*   As you earn rewards, claim your $THE farm rewards and $LISTA emission rewards by clicking the respective “Claim” buttons, stacking multiple yield streams effortlessly.

Benefits of Minting $clisBNB
============================

*   **Multiple Yield Streams**: Combine ThenaFi’s $THE rewards, Lista DAO’s $LISTA emissions, and Binance Launchpool rewards in one strategy.
*   **Capital Efficiency**: Use your LP tokens to mint $clisBNB without locking up your assets, retaining flexibility for other DeFi activities.

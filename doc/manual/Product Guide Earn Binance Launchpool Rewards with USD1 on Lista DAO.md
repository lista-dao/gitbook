### Product Guide: Earn Binance Launchpool Rewards with USD1 on Lista DAO

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*6UndumrTicUYhfIh)

Lista DAO’s integration with USD1, announced on May 16, 2025, brings new opportunities for users to maximize their DeFi returns. Among the three integrations — USD1 Vault, USD1 in CDP, and lisUSD/USD1 LP — this article focuses on the USD1 Vault, specifically how users holding USD1 can leverage it to earn Binance Launchpool rewards. By depositing USD1 into the bnb Vault and utilizing Lista DAO’s slisBNB or clisBNB mechanisms, you can amplify your rewards during Launchpool events.

This guide will walk you through the process, highlighting the benefits and risks of this strategy.

### Why Use USD1 for Binance Launchpool Rewards?

Binance Launchpool events offer users the chance to stake assets and earn new tokens as rewards, often with high APYs during the event period. Lista DAO’s integration with USD1 allows you to:

*   **Leverage USD1**: Use your USD1 holdings to access BNB-based assets through Lista DAO’s lending vaults for Launchpool participation.
*   **Earn High Yields**: Capture Launchpool rewards alongside staking and lending incentives.
*   **Capital Efficiency**: Borrow BNB against USD1 collateral to scale your exposure without selling your stablecoins.

This strategy is ideal for USD1 holders looking to participate in high-yield opportunities while maintaining stablecoin exposure.

#### Step-by-Step Guide: Earning Launchpool Rewards

1.  **Deposit USD1 into the bnb Vault**:

*   Visit [lista.org](https://lista.org) and connect your wallet (e.g., MetaMask or WalletConnect).
*   Navigate to the “Lending” section and select the bnb Vault.
*   Deposit your USD1 as collateral.

**2\. Borrow BNB**:

*   In the borrowing section, select BNB and borrow within your collateral limit.
*   Confirm the transaction. You now hold BNB, a liquid asset on BNB Chain.

**3\. Choose Your Mechanism: slisBNB or clisBNB**:

**Option 1: slisBNB**:

*   On Lista DAO, navigate to the liquid staking section.
*   Liquid stake your BNB to receive slisBNB, which earns staking rewards (5–10% APR). For example, 1 BNB yields ~1 slisBNB.

**Option 2: clisBNB**:

*   Deposit your BNB into Lista’s CDP vault to receive clisBNB, a collateral certificate that can be used in advanced strategies.
*   Confirm the transaction.

**5\. Participate in Binance Launchpool**:

*   Visit Binance Launchpool and locate the active event.
*   Stake your slisBNB or clisBNB in the Launchpool to earn new tokens. For example, staking 1 slisBNB might yield 10–20% APY in new tokens during the event period.
*   Monitor your rewards and claim them as they accrue.

### Benefits of This Strategy

*   **High-Yield Opportunities**: Earn Launchpool rewards alongside slisBNB staking APY and LISTA borrowing incentives.
*   **Stablecoin Safety**: Retain USD1 as collateral, minimizing exposure to volatile assets.
*   **Flexibility**: Use slisBNB or clisBNB to participate in various Launchpool events.

### Conclusion

Using USD1 to participate in Binance Launchpool rewards through Lista DAO’s bnb Vault is a high-yield strategy for stablecoin holders. By borrowing bnb and leveraging slisBNB or clisBNB, you can capture significant rewards while maintaining USD1 exposure.

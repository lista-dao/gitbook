We're excited to announce the latest upgrade to Lista Smart Lending - version 1.1 - a complete UI overhaul and a suite of new features designed to give users full visibility and control over their lending experience.


## **What's New in Smart Lending 1.1**


### **All-in-One Market Dashboard**

Managing your lending activity just got a whole lot clearer. Smart Lending 1.1 introduces a comprehensive dashboard that consolidates everything you need in one place. At a glance, you can now check out:



* Markets - a complete overview of all available lending/swapping markets
* Pool Types - although most pools are stable pools, we will add more V2 and V3 pools later
* TVL (Total Value Locked) - real-time insight into the liquidity and scale of each market
* 24-Hour Volume - track trading and lending activity over the past day
* APY - up-to-date yield rates so you always know what your assets are earning
* For each market, we're also adding more statistics and charts to help you get a better grasp of it

With this dashboard, you never need to switch between markets or piecing together data from different pages. Everything is listed out clearly right where you need it.




### **Personal Portfolio Dashboard**

Smart Lending 1.1 also introduces a dedicated Personal Dashboard - a new way for any wallet to monitor their own activity on Smart Lending. Connect your wallet and instantly access a full breakdown of your portfolio, including:



* Your Active Markets - see exactly where your assets are deployed
* Current Value - track the real-time worth of your positions
* Position Details - granular information on each of your liquidity provision/lending positions
* Share in Pool - understand your proportional stake within each market and calculate your earnings with more accuracy
* Total Profit - a clear summary of your cumulative earnings over time

Whether you're a first-time lender or a seasoned DeFi participant, this personal dashboard will help you stay on top of your performance within one page.

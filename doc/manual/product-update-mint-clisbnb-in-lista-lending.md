At Lista DAO, we’re committed to enhancing the DeFi experience for our users by introducing innovative features that maximize capital efficiency and flexibility. Lista DAO is excited to announce a significant update to **Lista Lending**: users can now automatically mint clisBNB when depositing slisBNB as collateral in Lista Lending.

This update aligns with Lista DAO’s mission to provide seamless, capital-efficient solutions for BNB Chain users, empowering you to unlock more value from your assets while participating in the DeFi ecosystem.

In this article, we’ll dive into the details of this update, explore its key features, walk you through the user experience, and highlight how you can leverage this enhancement to optimize your DeFi strategies.

Key Features of the Update
==========================

Here’s a breakdown of the key features introduced in this update:

1\. Automatic Minting of clisBNB
================================

When you deposit slisBNB as collateral into Lista Lending, clisBNB will now be automatically minted with the following details:

*   **Mint Ratio**: 1:0.99813. For example, depositing 1 slisBNB will allow the user to mint 0.99813. clisBNB.
*   **Minting Price**: Determined by the current exchange rate at the time of deposit, ensuring fairness and transparency.

This automation simplifies the process, saving you time while ensuring you can immediately utilize clisBNB in your DeFi strategies.

2\. Flexible Target Address for Minting
=======================================

We’ve introduced the ability to modify the target address where your clisBNB is minted:

*   **Default Behavior**: Initially, clisBNB is minted to your current wallet address.
*   **Customization**: You can change the target address at any time. After modification, all subsequently deposited slisBNB will mint clisBNB to the new address.
*   **Single Address Limitation**: Note that clisBNB can still only be minted to one address at a time and cannot be split across multiple addresses.

This feature provides greater flexibility, allowing you to direct clisBNB to a different wallet for specific strategies, such as yield farming or delegation.

3\. Automatic Burning on Withdrawal
===================================

When you withdraw your slisBNB collateral from Lista Lending, the corresponding amount of clisBNB will be automatically and proportionally burned:

*   For example, if you withdraw 1 slisBNB, the equivalent 0.99813 clisBNB will be burned.
*   This ensures the system remains balanced and prevents over-issuance of clisBNB, maintaining its value and integrity within the ecosystem.

*   **Flexibility**: The ability to modify the target address for minting gives you control over where your clisBNB is sent.

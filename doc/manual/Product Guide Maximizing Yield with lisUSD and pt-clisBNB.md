### Product Guide: Maximizing Yield with lisUSD and pt-clisBNB:

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*ZDmgzAtAR1mgeoO5)

This guide introduces a powerful strategy leveraging pt-clisBNB, a yield-bearing derivative, to amplify returns through a looping mechanism on Lista Lending. By utilizing BNB, slisBNB, and pt-clisBNB, users can borrow WBNB, liquid stake, and reinvest to maximize their DeFi potential.

This strategy is designed for users starting with BNB, guiding them to acquire pt-clisBNB, collateralize it to borrow WBNB, unwrap and liquid stake into slisBNB, and zap back into pt-clisBNB for a repeatable cycle. Let’s explore the strategy and provide a step-by-step guide to implement it.

### Why This Strategy?

This pt-clisBNB strategy combines Lista DAO’s liquid staking and lending capabilities to create a high-yield, capital-efficient loop, offering:

*   **Amplified Yields**: Earn staking rewards, lending incentives, and pt-clisBNB’s yield-to-maturity benefits.
*   **Capital Efficiency**: Leverage collateral to access liquidity without selling assets.
*   **Flexibility**: Use WBNB and lisUSD across DeFi protocols for diverse opportunities.
*   **Scalability**: The looping mechanism allows advanced users to scale exposure while managing risk.

With Lista Lending’s secure P2P lending and the innovative pt-clisBNB derivative, this strategy is ideal for DeFi enthusiasts aiming to optimize returns in the BNBFi ecosystem.

### The Strategy: Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Start with BNB and Acquire pt-clisBNB

Assuming you hold BNB, the first step is to convert it into slisBNB and use it to purchase pt-clisBNB.

1.  **Access Lista DAO**: Visit [lista.org](https://lista.org/) and connect your wallet (e.g., MetaMask or WalletConnect).
2.  **Liquid Stake BNB into slisBNB**: Navigate to the liquid staking section. Deposit your BNB to receive slisBNB.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*NK_UDlkeYRHuBzzX)

**3\. Buy pt-clisBNB**: Go to Pendle Finance’s market ([app.pendle.finance](https://app.pendle.finance/)), and search for clisBNB.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*iren5IFYcSSo5JIR)

4\. Swap your slisBNB for pt-clisBNB.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*lsaYoDBLd6708Hba)

**Note**: pt-clisBNB’s price may vary based on yield-to-maturity and market demand. Confirm the trade details to avoid slippage.

### Step 2: Collateralize pt-clisBNB on Lista Lending

Use pt-clisBNB as collateral to borrow WBNB, Lista Lending’s wrapped BNB token.

1.  **Deposit pt-clisBNB as Collateral**: On Lista DAO, navigate to Lista Lending section and click on the Lista BNB vault.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*3EdAsatIPLNjj4X2)

2\. Select pt-clisBNB and deposit the desired amount.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*bARYoFRBhRXX2e9l)

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*VLiDtISbmI2Zj-pb)

**3\. Borrow WBNB**: In the borrowing section, select WBNB and specify the amount within the collateral ratio. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.

**4\. Confirm Transaction**: You now hold WBNB, a liquid asset usable across BNB Chain DeFi protocols.

**Tip**: Borrowing on Lista Lending often includes LISTA token rewards, enhancing overall returns.

### Step 3: Unwrap WBNB to BNB

Convert WBNB back to native BNB to continue the looping strategy.

1.  **Access a DEX**: Visit a DEX like PancakeSwap ([pancakeswap.finance](https://pancakeswap.finance/))
2.  **Unwrap WBNB**: Swap your WBNB for BNB. This is typically a 1:1 conversion with minimal fees, as WBNB is a wrapped version of BNB.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*FgHJGoukDCB55vCe)

**3\. Confirm Transaction**: Ensure the swap is complete, and you now hold BNB in your wallet.

### Step 4: Liquid Stake BNB into slisBNB

Repeat the liquid staking process to convert BNB back into slisBNB.

1.  **Return to Liquid Staking**: On Lista DAO, go to the liquid staking section.
2.  **Stake BNB**: Deposit your BNB to receive slisBNB. For example, 1 BNB yields ~1 slisBNB, continuing to earn staking rewards.
3.  **Confirm Transaction**: Approve the staking transaction. You now hold slisBNB again.

### Step 5: Zap into pt-clisBNB

“Zap” your slisBNB back into pt-clisBNB to complete the loop and scale your position.

1.  **Buy pt-clisBNB Again**: Return to Pendle Finance market. Swap your slisBNB for pt-clisBNB, as in Step 1.
2.  **Confirm Transaction**: Finalize the trade. Your new pt-clisBNB can be collateralized to borrow more WBNB, restarting the cycle.

**Looping Benefit**: Each cycle increases your exposure to pt-clisBNB’s fixed yield and slisBNB’s staking rewards, amplifying returns. However, it also increases leverage and risk.

### Conclusion

The pt-clisBNB strategy leverages Lista Lending’s robust infrastructure to create a high-yield, capital-efficient loop. By starting with BNB, acquiring pt-clisBNB, collateralizing it to borrow WBNB, and reinvesting through liquid staking, users can amplify exposure to staking rewards, fixed yields, and LISTA incentives. This approach is ideal for advanced DeFi users comfortable with leverage and risk management.

### Understanding the potential of slisBNB and clisBNB

First things first, many of Lista DAO’s high-yield strategies start or end with converting BNB into slisBNB through liquid staking, then minting clisBNB for further Binance launchpool regards.

Begin by following these steps:

1.  [stake BNB on Lista DAO’s platform](https://lista.org/liquid-staking/BNB) to receive slisBNB, a yield-bearing token that earns staking rewards ( ~1–3% APR) while remaining liquid.
2.  Next, use slisBNB or BNB to mint clisBNB — either by [depositing slisBNB or BNB into Lista’s CDP](https://lista.org/cdp/dashboard), by [depositing slisBNB into Lista Lending](https://lista.org/lending/market/0x2bb68bc7f70186f3d4f16db6a19986df6c6cdea3e589c1ae3d30b56b0632c5ec), or [Mint slisBNB/BNB LP token](https://lista.org/rewards#thena_slisBNB_BNB_correlated_LP_STAKE_POOL). Depositing slisBNB into the CDP allows users to borrow our native stablecoin, lisUSD.
3.  Meanwhile, clisBNB allows users to participate in Binance launchpool rewards even when their assets are deposited as collateral on Lista DAO or Lista Lending.
4.  This flow unlocks capital efficiency and powers looping strategies, maximizing your returns in 2025.

### 1\. slisBNB Looping Strategy

**How It Works:**

1.  Acquire BNB and stake it on [Lista Liquid Staking](https://lista.org/liquid-staking/BNB) to receive slisBNB, a yield-bearing token that appreciates with BNB’s staking APR.
2.  [Use slisBNB as collateral](https://lista.org/lending/market/0x2bb68bc7f70186f3d4f16db6a19986df6c6cdea3e589c1ae3d30b56b0632c5ec) in Lista Lending to borrow more BNB.
3.  Repeat steps 1–2 to loop the strategy, amplifying your position.

**Estimated APY:** ~10% per loop (~18% from launchpool rewards and liquid staking rewards, minus a ~10% borrow rate).

**Max Loop Times APR:** With up to 28 loops, the APR can reach 100%+, depending on market conditions and borrowing rates.

**Risks:**

*   **Interest Rate Fluctuations:** Rising borrowing rates in Lista Lending can erode profits, especially when utilization rate goes above 90%, and borrowing rates will start increasing dramatically.
*   **Withdraw and repay time**: To repay every loop, the repaid asset needs to be BNB, when users convert slisBNB into BNB back again, remember there is a 7 day withdrawal time. Another way to convert slisBNB back to BNB is directly swap on pancakeswap, but users would need to be cautious of the exchange rate if they are swapping a large amount.

### 2\. Leveraging PT-clisBNB for Fixed-Yield Farming (Looping Strategy)

This PT-clisBNB strategy combines Lista DAO’s liquid staking and lending capabilities with Pendle Finance’s fixed-yield tokens to create a high-yield, capital-efficient loop.

**How It Works:**

1.  Acquire BNB and stake it on [Lista Liquid Staking](https://lista.org/liquid-staking/BNB) to receive slisBNB, a yield-bearing token that appreciates with BNB’s staking APR.
2.  Zap slisBNB for PT-clisBNB (Principal Token) on [Pendle Finance](https://app.pendle.finance/trade/pools/0xbd577ddabb5a1672d3c786726b87a175de652b96/zap/in?chain=bnbchain), locking in a fixed yield.
3.  [Use PT-clisBNB as collateral](https://lista.org/lending/market/0x0e9ce37ed19824e0698b8cf1855bef55cefdc82f37c321c3812d90135f476709) in Lista Lending to borrow more BNB.
4.  Repeat steps 1–3 to loop the strategy, amplifying your position.

**Estimated APY:** ~8–10% per loop (~8–10% fixed yield from PT-clisBNB ).

**Max Loop Times APR:** With up to 4 loops, the APR can reach ~30–40 %, depending on market conditions and borrowing rates.

**Why It Stands Out:** PT-clisBNB offers predictable returns through Pendle’s fixed-yield mechanism, while borrowing against it in Lista Lending amplifies yield. This strategy is stable compared to volatile asset farming, making it ideal for risk-averse investors seeking consistent returns.

**Risks:**

*   **Interest Rate Fluctuations:** Rising borrowing rates in Lista Lending can erode profits, especially when utilization rate goes above 90%, and borrowing rates will start increasing dramatically.
*   **Liquidation Risk:** Each loop increases leverage, raising the risk of liquidation if collateral (PT-clisBNB) value drops or borrowed BNB value rises.
*   **Maturity date:** The Pendle PT assets have maturity dates so users need to have in mind to repay on maturity date and roll over to the new Pendle pool for the next fund.

### 3\. PT-USDe for Stablecoin Yield Farming (Looping Strategy)

This strategy uses Pendle Finance’s PT-usde (Principal Token for USDE stablecoin) to farm stable yields, amplified through Lista Lending’s borrowing mechanism with USD1 vault or USDT vault.

**How It Works:**

1.  Acquire USDe and [zap it for PT-usde on Pendle Finance](https://app.pendle.finance/trade/pools/0x6d98a2b6cdbf44939362a3e99793339ba2016af4/zap/in?chain=ethereum) to lock in a fixed yield.
2.  [Use PT-usde as collateral](https://lista.org/lending/market/0x023110cdfe45b5d2e6dc738934fb9946b319fb4b4c767767c281743d0ab3297c) in Lista Lending to borrow USD1 or USDT.
3.  Convert borrowed assets (USD1 or USDT) back to USDe and repeat steps 1–2 to loop the strategy.

**Estimated APY:** 7–8% per loop (7–8% fixed yield from PT-usde).

**Max Loop Times APR:** Up to 4 loops can yield ~22–30%, depending on borrowing rates and LLTV (Liquidation Loan to Value) ratio from each loop. (The higher the LLTV ratio, the higher the net APR, but it also increases the chance of liquidation).

**Why It Stands Out:** PT-USDe offers stable returns with minimal exposure to crypto volatility, making it a safer option for conservative yield farmers. Looping the stablecoins via Lista Lending enhances capital efficiency without significant price risk.

**Risks:**

*   **Stablecoin Depeg Risk:** If USDe or USD1 loses its peg, collateral or borrowed asset value could destabilize.
*   **Interest Rate Volatility:** Rising borrowing rates in Lista Lending can reduce profitability, especially when utilization rate goes above 90%.
*   **Maturity date:** The Pendle PT assets have maturity dates so users need to have in mind to repay on maturity date and roll over to the new Pendle pool for the next fund.

### 4\. Earning Binance Launchpool Rewards with USD1

This strategy involves collateralising USD1 on Lista lending to borrow BNB, followed by liquid staking BNB into slisBNB, and participating in Binance Launchpool opportunities.

**How It Works:**

1.  [Deposit USD1](https://lista.org/lending/market/0xd384584abf6504425c9873f34a63372625d46cd1f2e79aeedc77475cacaca922) (a stablecoin) into the BNB Market in Lista Lending.
2.  Borrow BNB against USD1 collateral.
3.  [Liquid stake BNB](https://lista.org/liquid-staking/BNB) into slisBNB, and earn liquid staking rewards + Binance Launchpool rewards. Do note that Binance MPC wallet is required for Launchpool rewards.
4.  Alternatively, loop your strategy by swapping slisBNB into PT-clisBNB on Pendle, collateralise PT-clisBNB to borrow BNB, and repeat step 3.

**Estimated APR:** ~18% (BNB Liquid staking + Binance Launchpool rewards).

**Max Loop Times APR:** N/A (no looping, as this is a single-deposit strategy).

**Why It Stands Out:** This strategy is simple and low-risk, ideal for beginners or those seeking a stable return yet attractive return with very low risks.

**Risks:**

*   **USD1 depegging risks**: Although unlikely, if USD1 de-pegs, it may result in loan liquidation.
*   **Interest Rate Volatility:** Rising borrowing rates in Lista Lending can reduce profitability, especially when utilization rate goes above 90%.

### 5\. Collateralise BTC, ETH or other assets to borrow BNB for staking Yields + Launch pool rewards

This strategy involves collateralising BTC or ETH on Lista Lending to borrow BNB and earn consistent staking yields + Binance launch pool rewards without looping.

**How It Works:**

1.  [Deposit BTC](https://lista.org/lending/market/0x9a7d48f4d5a39353ff9d34c4cefc2dc933bcc11e8be1a503db0910678763c394) or ETH into Lista Lending markets to borrow BNB.
2.  Liquid stake BNB into slisBNB on Lista DAO’s BNB liquid staking platform
3.  Earn staking rewards for BNB + Binance launchpool rewards while holding slisBNB.

**Estimated APR:** 17–18% (driven by slisBNB APR minus borrow rates for BNB using BTC/ETH collateral).

**Max Loop Times APR:** N/A (no looping, as this is a single-deposit strategy).

**Why It Stands Out:** Borrowing BNB using BTC or ETH offers exposure to high-value assets with lower volatility compared to altcoins. It’s a passive strategy suitable for long-term holders seeking moderate yields in BNB while retaining their ownership in ETH or BTC.

**Risks:**

*   **Price Volatility:** BTC or ETH price drops could result in liquidation, though lending itself is low-risk.

### 6\. Borrow USDT/USD1/lisUSD to USR for Airdrop Rewards (CDP or Lista Lending Strategy)

This strategy uses Lista DAO’s CDP system or lending protocol to borrow stablecoins and participate in USR-related airdrop farming.

**How It Works:**

1.  Deposit collateral (e.g., BNB, slisBNB, ETH) into Lista’s CDP system or Lending protocol.
2.  Borrow USDT, USD1, or lisUSD against the collateral.
3.  Use borrowed stablecoins to provide liquidity or stake in USR-related pools (e.g., on supported DEXes) to earn airdrop rewards.

**Estimated APR:** ~8–10% (from lending/staking rewards, plus variable airdrop rewards in LISTA or other tokens).

**Max Loop Times APR:** N/A (no looping, as airdrop farming is a single-cycle strategy).

**Why It Stands Out:** This strategy combines stablecoin borrowing with airdrop farming, offering both predictable yields and potential high-value rewards. It’s ideal for users seeking to diversify income streams within Lista DAO’s ecosystem.

**Risks:**

*   **Collateral Liquidation Risk:** If collateral value drops below the loan threshold, assets may be liquidated via Lista’s Dutch auction process.
*   **Airdrop Reward Uncertainty:** Airdrop rewards are speculative and may not materialize as expected.

### 7\. Loop BNB Vault on Lista Lending

This is a simple one step strategy where users can simply deposit BNB into our latest [Loop BNB vault](https://lista.org/lending/vault/0xab251dc87dc313649d024bd69b34c8e7690ce1fc#vault) on Lista Lending and get direct exposure to attractive slisBNB lending yields on Lista Lending.

**Estimated APR**: ~7.5–8%

**Max Loop times** **APR**: N/A

**Why It Stands Out:** This is a simple strategy for passive users who wants to earn attractive and safe returns on their BNB holdings. The reason why the vault is able to offer such a competitive lending yield is due to how the slisBNB/BNB market operates. Borrowers use looping strategies to participate in Binance Launchpool multiple times, generating even higher yields with each loop. Because these borrowers expect greater profits from their strategies, they’re willing to pay a higher borrowing rate. As a result, if you are a supplier, you directly benefit from these elevated borrowing rates and can capture higher APYs on your BNB. Through the Loop BNB vault, you gain focused exposure to BNBFi’s most active and high-demand market, the slisBNB/BNB looping strategy.

**Risks:**

*   **BNB price Risk:** During times of volatility, the price of BNB may drop and this drop in value is borne by the user.

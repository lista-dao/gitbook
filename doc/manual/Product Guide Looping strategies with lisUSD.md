### Product Guide: Looping strategies with lisUSD

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*Cv38a7pWoeIcvn1_)

As the decentralized finance (DeFi) landscape evolves, Lista DAO continues to innovate, offering users powerful tools to maximize capital efficiency and yield opportunities. With our flagship stablecoin, lisUSD, and the recent integration of Lista Lending with Euler Finance’s modular vaults, users can now unlock sophisticated strategies to optimize their assets.

This guide outlines a step-by-step strategy to leverage Lista Lending and Euler vaults, enabling you to borrow, stake, and trade with enhanced liquidity and yield potential.

This strategy involves collateralizing assets to borrow BNB on Lista Lending, liquid staking BNB into slisBNB, borrowing lisUSD, and utilizing Euler vaults to access USDC liquidity for trading or supplying lisUSD additional yields.

Additionally, users can collateralize assets on Euler to borrow USDC as debt, enhancing flexibility. Let’s dive into the strategy and guide.

### Why This Strategy?

This approach combines the strengths of Lista DAO’s lending and liquid staking solutions with Euler Finance’s modular vault system, offering:

*   **Capital Efficiency**: Leverage collateral to access liquidity without selling assets.
*   **Yield Opportunities**: Earn staking rewards, lending yields, and trading profits.
*   **Flexibility**: Use lisUSD and USDC across DeFi ecosystems for diverse strategies.
*   **Stability**: lisUSD’s over-collateralized design ensures reliability, while Euler’s vaults provide secure liquidity.

### The Strategy: Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Collateralize Assets to Borrow BNB on Lista Lending

Lista Lending allows users to deposit various assets as collateral to borrow BNB, providing scalable liquidity within the BNB Chain ecosystem.

1.  **Visit Lista DAO**: Go to lista.org and connect your wallet (e.g., MetaMask or WalletConnect).
2.  **Deposit Collateral**: Navigate to the Lista Lending page, and click on “Lista BNB vault”

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*YPJ1J5HUjNURJ_no)

**3\. Borrow BNB**:From the selection, choose a vault of your liking to deposit collateral and borrow WBNB against. In this example, we will be using solvBTC/WBNB as an example.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*XP3AR-Cd17fygePE)

### Step 2: Liquid Stake BNB into slisBNB

Lista DAO’s liquid staking solution lets you stake BNB to earn rewards while maintaining liquidity through slisBNB, a yield-bearing token.

1.  **Access Liquid Staking**: On the Lista DAO platform, navigate to the liquid staking section.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*Gjdsm2NHLSG0710j)

**2\. Stake BNB**: Deposit your borrowed BNB to receive slisBNB. For example, staking 1 BNB yields approximately 1 slisBNB, which appreciates against BNB based on staking APR.

**3\. Confirm Transaction**: Approve the staking transaction in your wallet. Your slisBNB is now ready for further use.

**Benefit**: slisBNB earns passive staking rewards and can be used as collateral or in DeFi protocols, enhancing flexibility.

### Step 3: Collateralize slisBNB to Borrow lisUSD

With slisBNB, you can borrow lisUSD, Lista DAO’s decentralized stablecoin, to access stable liquidity.

1.  **Deposit slisBNB as Collateral**: Return to the homepage on Lista DAO. Select borrow lisUSD and click on the slisBNB as collateral option.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*se4EOk7H4XEqOHEG)

**2\. Borrow lisUSD**: In the borrowing section, choose lisUSD and specify the amount based on your collateral value.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*s6t6-CZfXOFRoPVG)

**3\. Confirm Transaction**: Finalize the borrowing transaction. You now hold lisUSD, a stable asset for DeFi activities.

### Step 4: Utilize lisUSD on Euler Vaults for USDC Liquidity or Yield

Euler Finance’s modular vaults, integrated with Lista DAO, offer two powerful options: supplying lisUSD for up to 20% APY or collateralizing lisUSD to borrow USDC for trading.

#### Option 1: Supply lisUSD for Yield

1.  **Access Euler Finance**: Visit euler.finance and connect your wallet.
2.  **Locate lisUSD Vault**: Find the lisUSD vault of your choosing.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*S1-CmHJkJnT8FuMG)

**3\. Supply lisUSD**: Deposit your lisUSD into the vault. For example, supplying 1,000 lisUSD can earn up to ~20.96% APY, generating passive income under the Re7 Labs Cluster lending vault.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*a9T5uhzDvMFKodAB)

**4\. Confirm Transaction**: Approve the deposit. Your lisUSD now earns yield while remaining accessible.

**Advantage**: This is a low-risk option for earning consistent returns on stablecoin holdings.

#### Option 2: Collateralize lisUSD to Borrow USDC

1.  **Select lisUSD as Collateral**: On Euler, deposit lisUSD into a vault supporting USDC borrowing.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*IvGirJ17LSAlOqHy)

**2\. Borrow USDC**: Choose USDC and borrow based on your collateral value.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/0*w4i6tf9N1UrN1OTX)

**3\. Confirm Transaction**: Finalize the borrowing. Use USDC for trading, liquidity provision, or other DeFi strategies.

**Why Borrow as Debt?**: Borrowing USDC as debt allows you to maintain lisUSD exposure while accessing liquid capital for high-yield opportunities, such as trading or providing liquidity on DEXs.

# bStocks

Lista Lending supports tokenized equities (bStocks) as collateral, allowing users to borrow stablecoins against on-chain stock exposure while retaining their underlying positions.

The first batch of bStocks available as collateral are: CRCLB, MUB, NVDAB, SNDKB, TSLAB, and SPCXB. Available loans against these assets are: USDT, USD1, U, and lisUSD.

bStocks Lending works the same as Lista Lending, available 7/24. bStocks Lending gets its price feeds from Atlas Oracle.

Please note that Lista reserves the right to pause certain functions for certain markets in case of emergency.

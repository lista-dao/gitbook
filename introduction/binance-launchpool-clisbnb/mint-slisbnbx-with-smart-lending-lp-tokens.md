# Mint slisBNBx with Smart Lending LP Tokens

Starting now, providing BNB/slisBNB liquidity via Lista Smart Lending will also be eligible for minting slisBNBx.

Currently, some users may experience problems when depositing slisBNB as collateral:

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (69).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

To resolve this issue, click on “Manage slisBNBx” at the bottom or visit [this page](https://lista.org/lending/delegate?utm_source=gitbook&utm_medium=article&utm_campaign=mint-slisbnbx-with-smart-lending-lp-tokens):

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (70).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

On our slisBNBx delegation page, delegate your slisBNBx to any other wallet that is controlled by you:

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (71).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Then, head back to the previous page where you wished to deposit slisBNB as collateral and make a deposit/withdrawal of any amount. Now, your slisBNBx will be delegated to your current wallet.
